Recent Reading

On Writing Well by William Zinsser
An excellent guide to writing nonfiction. I make it a point to read this one at least every year.
Getting Things Done by David Allen
The ultimate masterwork of modern productivity. If you're too busy to read this book, that's why you need to read it.
Red Storm Rising by Tom Clancy
A wonderful tale of tension and terror in the late Soviet era. Dated, but still wonderful.

Recent Albums

Bridge Over Troubled Water (1970, Columbia)
The last Simon & Garfunkel album, and in many ways, the best.
Still Crazy After All These Years (1975, Columbia)
My favorite Paul Simon album, along with Graceland.
Speakerboxxx/The Love Below (2004, Arista)
A mammoth OutKast album, still as fresh and relevant as it was 13 years ago.
Lungs (2009, Moshi Moshi)
Florence + the Machine's freshman effort and one of my favorite albums ever pressed. ♥
